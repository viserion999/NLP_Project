## [Human Survey] 
#### Weightage of different components in evaluation metric

We collected human judgments on multiple lyrical quality dimensions, including phonetic flow, rhythmic flow, emotional progression (emotion arc), rhyme consistency, hook quality, textual diversity, and thematic coherence. Each component was rated on a 10-point scale to capture its relative importance in perceived lyric quality. These annotations were used to calibrate and validate the weighting of different components in our evaluation framework.
